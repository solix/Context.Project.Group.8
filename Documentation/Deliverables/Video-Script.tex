For submission to the CHI Play competition, we will create a video showcasing our video game Taxi Trouble. The video will consist of screen-captured gameplay.\\\\
The video will start by stating that we are a team of students and that we have set out to create a game to collectively entertain a large group of people.\\\\
Following the introduction, the video will show the main gameplay elements for the driver:
\begin{itemize}
\item Pick up a passenger
\item Race to deliver a passenger on time
\end{itemize}
Then, the video will show the importance of the navigator with the following gameplay elements:
\begin{itemize}
\item Help your driver navigate around the large city
\item Plan out an optimal route for your driver
\item Activate power-ups
\end{itemize}
After this, the video will showcase the competitive team gameplay:
\begin{itemize}
\item Play against other teams
\item Steal passengers in order to win
\end{itemize}
